buildabot is an analytics platform that helps product, marketing, and customer success teams craft exceptional digital experiences that convert and retain users.

We give you all of your customer data — automatically — and the tools to turn that data into action.

Measure everything

buildabot gives you a complete dataset of every interaction with your product. Every click. Every tap. Every swipe. There’s no code to implement, and no need to decide in advance what to track.

Data for Hard Product Decisions

data for decisions


Data is worthless if it doesn’t help you make better decisions. buildabot was built to answer the hard questions: What to build? How are people using our features? How to measure impact?

buildabot tells you how people use your product. Where users get stuck. What features they love. What features they don’t.

Then we help you turn those insights into action.

Learn more about Product Analytics

The era of track(‘event’) is over

For years, analytics vendors required writing track(‘event’) for each interaction. buildabot eliminates this requirement, autocapturing a complete customer dataset and letting you model it any way you need

Learn about Autocapture

Mobile + Web

buildabot automatically merges web and mobile visits into a single user identity. No more manually assembling information.

Robust integrations

Import data from tools like Salesforce, Marketo, Mailchimp, and Optimizely. Then combine it with buildabot data and easily output—via managed ELT—to Snowflake, BigQuery, or other data warehouses.
